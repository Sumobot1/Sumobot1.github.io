About Me

I’m currently in my 2A term of Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo. For my 1A coop term, I had the opportunity to work as a Software Support Specialist for Videostream, a company specializing in streaming media from your laptop to your Chromecast. For my 1B coop term, I worked as a Software Developer for BuildDirect, an online marketplace, similar to Amazon, but for larger items like flooring and appliances. This term, I’m taking Statistical Analysis (MTE 201), Ordinary Differential Equations (MTE 202), Mechanics of Deformable Solids (MTE 219), Digital Logic (MTE 262), Dynamics (SYDE 182), and Microeconomics (ECON 101). I love learning new skills, and building cool stuff in both hardware and software.

In my spare time, I like to play computer games with friends, play guitar, listen to music, and spend time with family. Some of my favourite computer games include: Counter Strike Global Offensive, Team Fortress 2, Life is Strange, Bioshock Infinite, Saint's Row the Third, and Saints Row IV. Some of my favourite artists and bands include Ed Sheeran, Against the Current, Hedley, and Christina Grimmie.

Staying healthy is important. In high school, I was on the Cross Country, Swim, and Track and Field Teams. In university I continue to try to stay active by swimming in the UW pool, and using the workout facilities in the athletic complex. Outside of school, I practice Karate and have attained my Black Belt.

I love to travel. I have been to many places with family and friends, including Canmore, Alberta; San Diego, California; Cape Canaveral and Orlando, Florida; Pikes Peak, Colorado, New Brunswick and a number of islands throughout the Caribbean. I find travelling is an incredible opportunity to explore new sights, and relax with friends and family. In the summer of my Grade 12 year, I was fortunate to see a Delta IV Medium launch out of Cape Canaveral!

Stop Motion Lego Film

In my Grade 10 history class, one of my projects was to investigate Prohibition in the USA in the 1920s. I chose to do this by making a stop motion Lego film. My friend and I made sets out of Lego, and then alternated between taking pictures and changing positions. I also wanted to add a Star Trek element to the video, so I took some pictures of my Mega Blocks model of the Enterprise D. Each slide was created manually in Powerpoint. I then edited the video together using Adobe Premier Elements. My family and I did the voice acting on the project. The video is quite long, so I have taken the liberty of compiling a few clips from it to put on my website.

Hadouken GIF

In media club, I decided I wanted to make a Hadouken GIF, like what you see in streetfighter. I got two of my friends to act out the scene, while I took pictures of them. I then edited each picture individually in Photoshop, and exported the photoshop project as a GIF.

Photography

By the end of Grade 12, my family and I decided I needed a good phone for university. I decided to get the LG G4 so that I could document some of the places I have been, and hopefully learn some more about photography. Since I got the camera, I have travelled to both Cape Canaveral, Florida and Moncton, New Brunswick. These are some pictures from my trips.
